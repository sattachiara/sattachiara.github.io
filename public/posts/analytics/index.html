Before the UK

Until 2015

Born and bred in Sardinia, I moved to the UK back in 2015. I am now looking for my next adventure. After spending several years as freelancer tutor of English in Italy, I decided to move to the UK to implement my language skills but also seek for further professional opportunities.

English language has always been a top priority in my development. I started learning it in primary school, but it was only when teenager and with the advent of MTV music videos that I truly started to appreciate it.

Prior to the UK, I worked since I was 16 in very different roles: from working in my family bakery every summer, to cleaning VIP villas during summertime, my career as a professional started during the university in Cagliari. I took an internship at a local paint factory in my hometown that was expanding their operations beyond the Italian border. For this company, I offered interpret and translation services from English/French to Italian and helped with viva negotiations during conference calls.

From 2011 to 2015, while studying for my bachelor’s degree in foreign languages and communication, I started to offer study support and tutoring to young adults with a particular focus to English language.

Netflix and real time streaming services were not particularly big at the time, and being a super fan of US and British TV series that were not available on TV yet, I started to volunteer as translator for dubbing platforms that were providing subtitles for TV series. I was particularly fascinated by the cultural challenges posed by translation and started to research into the industry behind the translation of film titles in Italy, which most of the time translated and moved the title of the film to a completely different meaning.
